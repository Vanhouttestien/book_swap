About us

Reading supports children’s development. It opens their mind to learn new things and sparks creativity. It creates opportunities to see the world in a different way. As a parent it is also a fun activity to bond with your little one! But as an expat it is not always easy to find books in your mothertongue. Kids Book Swap Organises events in Stockholm to give children the opportunity to swap their own books. Of course if you just want to discover a new language you are also welcome!

Currently we have three language namely English, Dutch and French and 3 age groups (0-6, 6-12 and 12+) for every language.

The events find place in Årsta bibliotek and entrance is free. Fika is also provided. Hope to see you soon.
