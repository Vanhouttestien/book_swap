About us

Reading for childeren is a fun bonding activity and supports their development. It opens their mind to learn new things and sparks creativity it creates opportunities to see the world in a differnt way. But as a expats it isn't always easy to to find children's books in their mother tongue. Kids book swap organises events in Stockholm to give childeren the opportunity to swap their own books.

Currently we have three language namely English, Dutch and French and 3 age groups (0-6, 6-12 and 12+) for every language.
